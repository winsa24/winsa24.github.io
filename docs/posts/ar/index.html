Xperience of Virtual Plants (XVP)

November 15, 2020     15 s   AR Develop  

Based on Unity + Vuforia, created a AR plants guide book to help users learn rare plants. The users can scan a target image and he will see a 3D models. He could also interact with the model by clicking either the virtual buttons or screen buttons. Check the video to better learn our project.
